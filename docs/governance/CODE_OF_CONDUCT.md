#Code of Conduct

> **Language**: English | [中文](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.zh-CN.md)

The AgentNexus community wants to maintain a collaborative atmosphere that is professional, friendly, and problem-solving oriented.

## Expected Behavior

- Respect different backgrounds, experience levels and perspectives.
- Discussion focuses on facts, code, design, and user implications.
- Give a reproducible problem description and verifiable technical arguments.
- Accept the trade-offs maintainers make for the sake of project scope, quality, and security.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks, harassment, discrimination, threats or persistent provocation.
- Publish other people's private information, credentials, tokens, vulnerability exploit details or unauthorized data.
- Repeatedly swipe the screen, go off-topic, or maliciously consume maintenance resources in Issues, PRs, or discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers can edit or delete inappropriate content, close discussions, and restrict participants temporarily or permanently. For security vulnerability disclosure, please follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
