Delete a Cheers Account

Delete an accessible account

Sign in to Cheers, open Settings - Account, choose Delete account, and complete the recent-authentication confirmation. The control is available in the web, macOS, and iOS apps.

If you cannot sign in

Email tocheers@icloud.com from the address associated with the account. Do not send a password, OAuth code, access token, recovery code, private key, or provisioning profile.

What deletion does

Deletion revokes active sessions, device registrations, and linked identity credentials; anonymizes the profile; disables owned bots; and transfers or archives shared spaces. Historical messages may remain attributed to "Deleted User" to preserve other participants' conversations. Encrypted backups expire through normal rotation, subject to legal requirements.

For more detail, read the Privacy Policy or contact Cheers Support.
